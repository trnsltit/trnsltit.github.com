We've got what you need!

The "Translate It” app facilitates the communication of simple questions and phrases in multiple languages, with an emphasis on customizability and user-defined content. In this, it supports those providing humanitarian aid, medical services and governmental assistance, as well as newcomers themselves.

Features

Works Offline

You can fetch pre-made recordings online, but the application continues to work even when there is no connection available.

Add new recordings

You have the ability to add a new recording anytime, anywhere.

Compatible

It runs on multiple different devices, directly in a browser.

Organize

Organize your recordings in categories and folders to find them fast.
